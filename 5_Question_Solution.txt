DOCTYPE DECLARATION :

The DOCTYPE declaration is a special instruction that helps browser render the webp page correctly. Its primary goal is to define the rules that the markup language must follow. The most common declaration "DOCTYPE html" , indicates the use of "HTML5" , the latest version of html .

Example of a Doctype declaration for HTML5:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
    <title> DOCTYPE declaration </title>
</head>
<body>
    <!-- html content/element goes here  -->
</body>
</html>
